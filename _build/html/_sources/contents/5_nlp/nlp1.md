## **What is NLP (Natural Language Processing)?**
- **NLP** stands for **Natural Language Processing**.
- It enables computers to **analyze, understand, and derive meaning** from human languages (e.g., English, Spanish, Hindi).
- NLP leverages **machine learning algorithms** to process **speech and text**.

### **Why is NLP Popular?**
- The popularity of NLP is growing **every day**.
- NLP enables computers to understand human language **in both text and speech**.
- **Big data** enhances machine-human communication.
- **AI-powered systems (like robots)** can interpret human instructions in natural language (e.g., English).

---
## **Key Components of NLP**
There are **two main components** of NLP:

1. **Syntax**
2. **Semantic Analysis**

### **1. Syntax**
- Syntax refers to **the arrangement of words in sentences** and grammar rules.
- NLP uses syntax analysis to extract meaning based on grammatical rules.
- Common syntax techniques:
  - **Parsing**
  - **Word segmentation**
  - **Sentence breaking**
  - **Morphological segmentation**
  - **Stemming**

### **2. Semantics**
- Semantics deals with **the meaning of words and sentences**.
- NLP algorithms analyze and determine **meaning and structure**.
- Common semantic techniques:
  - **Word meaning extraction**
  - **Named Entity Recognition (NER)**
  - **Natural Language Generation (NLG)**

## **Role of Deep Learning in NLP**
- **Modern NLP heavily relies on deep learning**.
- Deep learning models improve NLP by recognizing **patterns in data**.
- These models require **large amounts of labeled data** to train effectively.
