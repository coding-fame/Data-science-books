Job Interview Conversation:#

Characters#

  • Gajaanana (YOU): A job seeker looking for opportunities.

  • Daniel: HR recruiter from Capgemini Company.

1. HR Recruiter: Initial Contact#

Daniel:
Can I speak to Gajaanana?

Gajaanana:
Yes, Gajaanana speaking. May I know who is on the line?

2. Introductions#

Daniel:
This is Daniel speaking from Capgemini company. I’m from the HR department. I got your profile from the job portal. We have an opening for a Data Scientist role.

Gajaanana:
Hi Daniel, good morning. How are you?

3. Recruiter’s Response#

Daniel:
I’m doing well, Gajaanana. How are you?

Gajaanana:
I’m doing great, Daniel. Thanks for asking. Let’s continue the discussion.

4. Communicating with the Recruiter#

  • Do not address the recruiter as “Sir/Madam.”

  • Use their name instead for a more casual and respectful tone.

5. Job Opening Discussion#

Daniel:
We have job openings and have shortlisted your profile. Is this a good time to talk?

Gajaanana:
That’s good to hear, Daniel. Yes, now is a good time to discuss.

Alternatively:
Yes, it is the right time, but can you please wait for 10 seconds while I get ready?

6. Experience and Relevant Skills#

Daniel:
How many years of experience do you have?

Gajaanana:
I have a total of 3.5 years of experience.

7. Relevant Experience in Data Science#

Daniel:
How many years of experience do you have in Data Science?

Gajaanana:
I have 3 years of experience in Data Science.

Alternatively:
My total experience and relevant experience are the same.

Alternatively:
I have 3 years in Data Science, and the rest of my experience is in another domain.

8. Current Company#

Daniel:
In which company are you currently working?

Gajaanana:
Currently, I’m working at Wipro.

9. Employment Type (Permanent or Contract)#

Daniel:
Is your role permanent or contract-based?

Gajaanana:
Yes, it’s a permanent role.

Alternatively:
No, it’s not a permanent role. I’m working on a contract/project basis at Wipro.

10. Payroll/Company Name#

Daniel:
What is your payroll/permanent company?

Gajaanana:
My payroll company is ABC Company.

11. Current Salary#

Daniel:
What is your current CTC (Cost to Company)?

Gajaanana:
Currently, my CTC is 8.5 lakhs per annum.

12. Expected Salary#

Daniel:
What is your expected CTC?

Gajaanana:
I am expecting 12 lakhs per annum.

13. Salary Negotiability#

Daniel:
Is your expected salary negotiable?

Gajaanana:
No, Daniel, it is not negotiable.

14. Available Time for Interview#

Daniel:
When are you available for an interview?

Gajaanana:
I’m available throughout the week and looking for a change immediately.

15. Notice Period#

Daniel:
What is your notice period?

Gajaanana:
My notice period is 2 months.

Alternatively:
Officially, it’s 2 months, but I can manage to join within 1 month.

16. Joining Before the Notice Period#

Daniel:
How is it possible for you to join within 1 month?

Gajaanana:
I have a good relationship with my manager and can finish the Knowledge Transfer (KT) quickly.

17. Possible Joining Date#

Daniel:
Is it possible to join within 20 days?

Gajaanana:
Yes, I can try. If I join within 20 days, will I be eligible for a welcome bonus or joining bonus?

18. Recruiter’s Response#

Daniel:
Yes, we will provide a welcome bonus.

Alternatively:
Sorry, we are unable to provide a welcome bonus.

19. Final Response from Recruiter#

Daniel:
Thanks for sharing all the details.

Gajaanana:
Can you please drop me an email with the details of our discussion?

20. Email Follow-up#

Daniel:
Sure, I’ll send an email regarding the details. Can you reply to it?

Gajaanana:
I’m a bit busy with office work right now, but I’ll reply in some time. Thanks!
